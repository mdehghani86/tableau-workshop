THE FIRST 7 SECONDS

The Hook

BORING
"Hi, my name is..."
"So today I'll talk about..."
"Let me give some background..."
MAGNETIC
"What if I told you..."
"Last Tuesday at 2 AM..."
"97% of you will fail at this."

7 seconds. That's how long you have before your audience decides you're worth listening to.

WORKSHOP 1

Write Your Micro-Story

Grab a piece of paper. Write 3 sentences:

YOUR PROMPT

"A moment that changed how you see a tool, a person, or a problem."

Sentence 1: Set the scene — where, when, who Sentence 2: The unexpected thing that happened Sentence 3: How it changed you

After: 2-3 volunteers read theirs aloud

WORKSHOP 2

Pitch Your App
as a Story

Think of an app idea. Now pitch it as a story, not a feature list.

YOUR 60-SECOND PITCH
Hook — "What if..." or "Picture this..." (10 sec) The person — who has this problem? Name them. (15 sec) The struggle — what's broken today? (15 sec) Your app — what happens when they use it? (15 sec) The "after" — how is their life different? (5 sec)

Groups of 3 · 1 min each · Listener gives 1-word reaction

TIMER

SEE THE DIFFERENCE

Same Product. Different Impact.

FEATURE DUMP

"Our app reduces doctor wait times by 73%. We have 50K users and a 4.8-star rating. We integrate with 200+ insurance providers. Our AI triages symptoms in real time."

😐 "OK, sounds fine. Next slide?"
STORY

"Last Tuesday at 9 PM, Sarah's 2-year-old spiked a 104° fever. She opened our app, found a pediatrician in 6 minutes, and by 10 PM her son was being treated. No ER. No 3-hour wait. Just a mom and a doctor, connected instantly."

😮 "How do I invest?"

Same product. Same data. Only one makes you feel something.
