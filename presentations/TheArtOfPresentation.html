IN-CLASS ACTIVITY

The Three-Product Reveal

Look around you. Pick any object you can see right now. Present it as three separate revolutionary products, then reveal it's one device.

EXAMPLE

"A portable office... a weather-proof vault... a personal comfort system... these are not three products — this is one device."

Groups of 3–4 · The more mundane, the better · 2 min per pitch

PRINCIPLE 5

Sell Dreams, Not Features

The only question your audience asks: "Why should I care?"

LEVEL 1 — FEATURE

"Capacitive multi-touch display"

LEVEL 2 — SO WHAT?

"No stylus needed. Your finger is the input device."

LEVEL 3 — WHY SHOULD I CARE?

"It works like magic."

Always start here. Work backwards.

THE GOLDEN PATH
